About Anomalies

Our objective is to detect and analyze anomalies within various datasets to ensure data integrity and accuracy. Anomalies, often referred to as outliers, are data points that differ significantly from the rest of the data. Identifying these anomalies helps in understanding the underlying patterns and improving the performance of database systems.

The purpose of this system is to automate the detection of these anomalies, suggest potential corrections, and ultimately improve the overall quality of data within our databases. The system not only highlights anomalies but also provides actionable insights to address these irregularities.

Through continuous monitoring and analysis, our system aims to optimize database performance and ensure that all data adheres to expected norms and standards.
